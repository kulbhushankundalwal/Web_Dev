How does it work?

To provide unconditional help to the poor, needy, and homeless by providing food for free, the process works as follows -

1. List the food

User can list the food & it's description using the website.

2. Pickup the food

Any non profit organisation will pickup the food from the nearest location using the list.

3. Serve the food

The organisation will serve the food and happiness.
