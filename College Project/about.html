About Us

We provide the solution to reduce food wastage & feed the people

In India, nearly about 1000 people sleep without food. According to the latest survey, 1.3 billion tons of food are thrown as waste every year. The focus of this website is to reduce the amount of food wastage and stop hunger by providing food to the needy people.

Our Mission

In India, nearly about 1000 people sleep without food who are really unfortunate to get food and cause diseases like Starvation, Malnutrition, etc.
So the main aim of this website is to stop hunger and provide them with food.
